Our Story

Tarun Pandvia

Groom
Son of Ajay Pandvia and Sangeeta Pandvia, he is the eldest son in the Pandvia's family, and the most tallest of them all. He is little unconventional and experimental with his ideas and always wanting to stand out of the crowd.
A very hardworking and practical man, who wants to be on the top. A down to earth guy with big dreams. A perfectionist. A man who loves his family immensely. He is a guy of ambitions, a guy of persistence, and lover with huge heart.

Ashu Jain

Bride
Daughter of Surendra Kumar Jain and Anju Jain. She is also the eldest daughter of the Jain's family, and the kind of happy go lucky and cordial natured people, who are always looking to put smiles on people's face.
A girl with simplicity and small little dreams. She has her own little world and wants to make the best out of it. She is contained but also possessive and want explore new things. She is intelligent and hardworking in every aspect of her life. A bit emotional but practical at the same time.
